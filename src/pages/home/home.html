HISTORICAL BACKGROUND Gingoog is a Manobo word for good luck. The word implies good fortune, thus Gingoog means the city of Good Luck. Gingoog originated from a thriving native settlement of Manobo tribe in the area, known today as Barangay Daan-Lungsod. GETTING HERE Laguindingan airport (approx. 130 kms away) and Butuan City airport (approx. 75 kms away) have daily flights to and from Manila through Philippine Airlines and Cebu Pacific Air.
